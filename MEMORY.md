- **Voice prompt v1+v2 (commits `e95fe2d` + `b647bb7`, 2026-05-27).** Six new behavioral rules in voice channel framing: (1) **Brevity** — match reply length to question size, 15-sec speech ceiling, stop reciting hours/numbers unprompted, don't summarize what caller just said. (2) **Voicemail intent** — caller asks to leave a message → Claire invites + stays silent for Vapi recording + fires `flag_for_office_review` with summary so office gets email + can play back audio. (3) **Voicemail hang-up cue** — Claire tells caller "Once you're finished, you can hang up and the office will get back to you" so they're not left wondering when to disconnect. (4) **No fake transfers** — Claire never promises "please hold while I transfer you" (no warm-transfer wired); takes info + `flag_for_office_review` instead. (5) **One piece at a time on structured intake** — never bundle "name and phone"; ask one piece per turn, wait, process, move on. (6) **Name confirmation** — always repeat the name back; ask to spell only on ambiguous/multi-syllable/foreign-origin names (rule of thumb: bet $20 → just confirm; hesitating → ask to spell).
