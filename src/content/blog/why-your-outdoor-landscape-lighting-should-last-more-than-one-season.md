## Why Homeowners Should Invest in High-Quality Outdoor Lighting

When it comes to outdoor lighting, most homeowners initially gravitate toward the convenient, off-the-shelf kits from big box stores. These affordable landscape lighting systems may seem like a quick fix, but the reality is they often don't last more than a season or two.

At **TZ Electric**, we specialize in professional-grade lighting solutions that are built to last both in terms of performance and durability. Below, we'll walk you through the essential differences between low-cost kits and high-quality systems and explain why investing in premium materials makes all the difference.

## It All Starts With the Transformer

The **transformer** is the foundation of any landscape lighting system. It powers every fixture, making its reliability absolutely crucial. Budget lighting kits typically come with standard powder-coated steel transformers. While inexpensive, these boxes are prone to corrosion, rust, and failure especially after exposure to rain, snow, and sunlight.

## What Makes Our Transformers Different?

At TZ Electric, we exclusively install **Volt Lighting stainless steel transformers**. These are engineered to last for decades and include advanced features such as:

- **Built-in timers and photocells** for automated on/off functionality
- **Remote control compatibility**, including integration with smart home systems like Ring
- **Mobile app controls** that allow you to create customized lighting schedules from your phone

This level of control and durability simply isn't available with entry-level systems.

### Stakes That Actually Hold Up

Lighting stakes may seem insignificant, but they play a critical role in the longevity of your system. Most store-bought kits include flimsy plastic stakes with PVC threads that snap or degrade with minimal force especially when bumped by a lawnmower or trimmer.

## Why We Use Brass-Inserted Stakes

We use premium stakes equipped with **solid brass inserts**, which offer:

- **Superior strength and impact resistance**
- **Reusability**, allowing them to be repositioned without cracking
- **Extended lifespan** that far exceeds that of plastic stakes

These small upgrades make a big difference in overall system durability.

### Fixtures That Endure All Weather Conditions

Your landscape lighting fixtures face the full range of outdoor conditions from scorching heat to freezing temperatures and heavy rain. Standard steel or aluminum fixtures from retail kits tend to corrode or fade over time, leading to poor aesthetics and performance issues.

### Our Solution: Solid Brass Fixtures

All our fixtures are made from **all-brass construction**, offering:

- **Enhanced durability and resistance to rust and corrosion**
- **Long-term performance with no warping or fading**
- **A high-end appearance** that complements any landscaping style

Not only do these fixtures perform better, but they also **look better**, maintaining their finish and appeal for years to come.

### Reliable Wiring and Connections

Most DIY lighting kits use quick-clip connections with metal barbs that pierce into wires. These may work initially, but they're vulnerable to moisture, pests, and ground shifts all of which can cause shorts or complete lighting failures.

### Professional-Grade Connections That Last

Our installations feature robust and moisture-resistant components, including:

- **Weatherproof wire nuts** filled with gel for superior sealing
- **Gel-filled splice kits** to keep moisture out and prevent corrosion
- **Secure, long-lasting connections** designed to withstand 3 to 7+ years without maintenance

This attention to detail ensures your system keeps shining night after night.
